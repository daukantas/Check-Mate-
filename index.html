EVO STAR, EVOSTOCK 2016 - 2017 is the Leading European Event on Bio-Inspired Computation. April 2017 .Pilot project: the development of online tools for beginning teachers and their mentors. https://etendering.ted.europa.eu/cft/cft-display.html?cftId=1625

The overall objective of this contract is to contribute to the improvement of European-level policy development in education and training. The specific objective of this contract is to increase self-efficacy of beginning teachers to become effective professionals. It will involve the development of online peer networks for (a) beginning teachers and (b) their mentors, as well as (c) an online professional development course for mentors of student school teachers.

16 EvoCOMNET solicits contributions dealing with the application of ideas from natural processes and systems to the definition, analysis, and development of novel sequential, parallel, and distributed evolutionary computation approaches to the solution of problems of practical and theoretical interest in all domains related to network systems. Use and tuning of hybrid computational approaches based on evolutionary techniques are also welcome. 17 Topics of interest include, but are not limited to: 18
